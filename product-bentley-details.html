Information

Bentley is a British luxury automobile company which is a complete subsidiary of Volkswagen AG, a German company. The main objective of Bentley is to design, manufacture, engineer and supply sophisticated world class automobiles under the brand name of Bentley. This brand is a descendant of Rolls Royce Motors, which was acquired by Volkswagen AG in 1998. This model was built by order of Brunei on an exclusive basis. Specifications and technical details are not revealed. The Rapier was a 2-door 4-seat sports coupe, based on the Bentley Java, but with the 6750 cc twin-turbo engine of 542 bhp (cf the Bentley Camelot). The Rapier was designed by DRA, Design Research Association from Warwick, and built by Hawtal Whiting. The underframe was from the Continental R and was redesigned. Compared with the Continental R the front wheels were moved forward 180mm, the rear wheels 100mm and the engine 100mm. Bentley Rapier is priced 4.5 million USD
